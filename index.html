Pantheon of Nations: Game Basics

A Beginner's Guide to the World of Avatars

Introduction

Pantheon of Nations is a strategic board game set in a rich fantasy world. Players embody Avatars, powerful deities, and command their loyal Followers in epic battles.

Game Objective: The last Avatar standing wins. If your Avatar's health reaches 0, you are eliminated, and all your Followers are removed from the board.

Phases of Play

Each game round unfolds through distinct phases:

  • Zero Round: Skip Betting, Order, and End of Round Phases. Each unit performs one action (Movement or Recruit) without an order token.
    [Image/Animation of Zero Round timeline]
  • Betting Phase: Players secretly bid faith tokens. The highest bidder becomes the first player, determining turn order.
  • Order Phase: Each player must give one order to one unit, spending faith tokens.
  • Action Phase: Players take turns revealing order tokens and executing unit actions.
  • End of Round Phase: All status effects activate. Ability cards advance on the cooldown track. The active Blessing is discarded. A new round begins.
Orders

During the Order Phase, you can choose one of the following actions for your unit:

  • Movement
    • Move a unit a specific number of hexes.
    • Foot Followers: 1 hex.
    • Mounted Followers (including Elephant with Follower): up to 2 hexes.
    • Avatars: up to 3 hexes.
    • Cannot move through other units.
      • [Image/Animation of Hex Grid Movement Ranges]
  • Recruit
    • Place new Followers on your starting hex or two adjacent starting hexes.
    • Only on empty hexes.
      • [Image/Animation of Recruit hexes]
  • Attack
    • The primary action for dealing damage.
    • Swordsmen: 4 damage to adjacent hexes (melee).
      [Image/Animation of Swordsmen Attack Pattern]
    • Spearmen: 2 damage to adjacent and subsequent hexes (piercing damage).
      [Image/Animation of Spearmen Attack Pattern]
    • Archers: Damage depends on range (3 hexes = 3 damage, 2 hexes = 2 damage, 1 hex = 1 damage). Shoot in an arcing trajectory.
      [Image/Animation of Archers Attack Pattern]
    • Avatars: Damage to adjacent hexes, dependent on abilities.
    • Imaginary Targets: You can attack empty hexes to avoid damaging allies or activating Blood Shield.
    • Health: Tracked on the tile (Followers) or nation board (Avatars, Daimyo, Elephant).
  • Blood Shield
    • Reflects incoming Attacks back to the attacker.
    • Activates automatically upon Attack.
    • Protects only from Attacks, not Abilities (unless the Ability explicitly states it performs an Attack).
    • Activates automatically if the player cannot perform other orders.
    • Unit with Blood Shield takes damage equal to its Attack (Archers take 2 damage).
      • [Image/Animation of Blood Shield effect]
  • Ability
    • Using special unit effects.
    • Active Abilities: Require an Ability token and have a cooldown.
    • Triggers: Activate when specific conditions are met (e.g., Recruit, enemy enters zone, unit death) without tokens.
    • Trajectories:
      • Line of Sight: (blocked by units)
      • Arcing Shot: (not blocked)
      • Any Unit: (within radius, regardless of obstructions)
    • Target Area: Some abilities affect multiple hexes based on a pattern.
      [Image/Animation of Hex Grid Target Area]

      (SRC = Source Unit, EFF = Effect Area)

      *White hex (SRC) is the source unit, teal (EFF) is the effect area.

    • Electricity X: Deals chain damage. First unit takes X damage, adjacent unit takes X-1, etc. Max chain length X. Each unit damaged once.
Status Effects

Special conditions affecting units:

  • Ice (X tokens): Unit cannot perform orders while tokens are present. 1 token removed at End of Round. Player can remove 1 token by spending an order action.
    [Image/Animation of Ice effect on unit]
  • Fire: Token on units or hexes. If a unit steps on a hex with Fire, token moves to unit. At End of Round, each Fire token deals X damage and is discarded.
    [Image/Animation of Fire effect on unit/hex]
  • Bleeding: Unit takes X damage after order activation, then token discarded. Only one Bleeding token deals damage per order activation.
    [Image/Animation of Bleeding effect on unit]
  • Blight (X tokens): At End of Round, unit takes damage equal to Blight tokens, then 1 token removed. Damage decreases each round.
    [Image/Animation of Blight effect on unit]
  • Important: If a player loses, status effects they applied remain on other players.
Additional Abilities
  • Multi-Strike: Perform an Attack X times.
  • Piercing Attack: Attack is dealt to multiple hexes in a straight line, dealing damage to each hex.
  • Attraction/Repulsion: Move the chosen unit a specified number of hexes away from or towards the unit using the ability. Deal damage equal to the number of hexes moved.
Key Terms
  • Avatar: Physical embodiment of a deity.
  • Followers: Warriors representing various nations.
  • Unit: Both an Avatar and a Follower.
  • Line of Sight: A straight line between units, blocked by other units.
  • Arcing Shot: A straight line not blocked by other units.
